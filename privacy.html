Privacy Policy — 100numbers

Last updated: 13 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GargantuaLab ("we", "us") handles information in connection with the mobile game 100numbers (the "App"). By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

The App does not require an account and we do not ask for or collect any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or phone number).

Your game progress, settings, and premium status are stored locally on your device. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us. Removing the App deletes this local data.

2. Purchases

The App offers an optional in-app purchase (removal of advertising and additional content). Purchases are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not receive or store your payment details. Please refer to the applicable store's privacy policy for how your payment information is handled.

3. Advertising

The free version of the App may display advertising provided by third-party advertising networks (for example, AppLovin and its mediation partners). To serve and measure ads, these third parties may collect information such as a device advertising identifier and limited usage data. This data is collected and controlled by those third parties under their own privacy policies, not by us.

On iOS, personalized advertising uses Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework: you will be asked for permission, and you can decline. You can also limit ad tracking in your device settings at any time. If you purchase the ad-free version, advertising is disabled.

4. Children

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

5. Your rights

Because we do not collect personal data, we do not hold information tied to your identity. For data handled by advertising partners or the app stores, you may exercise your rights (including under the GDPR and similar laws) directly with those providers, or by using your device's privacy and ad settings.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
